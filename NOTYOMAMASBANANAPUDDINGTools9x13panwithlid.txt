NOT YO’ MAMA’S BANANA PUDDING Tools 9x13 pan with lid


Tools
- 9x13 Pan with Lid
-Kitchen Aid Paddle attachment
-2 Kitchen Aid mixing bowls

Ingredients
4 bananas, sliced
3 cups whole milk
1 box Nilla Wafers
2 small boxes instant FRENCH vanilla pudding
1 {8 oz.} pkg. cream cheese
1 {14oz.} can sweetened condensed milk
1 {16oz.} container Cool Whip

Mix milk and pudding mix using paddle until combined

In a separate bowl, mix together cream cheese & condensed milk until smooth.

Gradually fold in whipped cream.

Add the cream cheese mixture to the pudding mix & stir until well blended.

Refrigerate in bowl overnight

Next Day:

-Slice your bananas and layer between the gaps of the wafers.

-Pour half the pudding mix on top and smooth evenly

-Lay another layer of whole Nilla wafers and bananas

-Pour remanding half of pudding on top and smooth evenly

- add a few sliced bananas on top and cover them with a whole Nilla wafer

-add crushed Nilla wafers on top to finish

-cover and let set for an hour in the fridge before serving.
