PIZZA GRILLED CHEESE


Prep Time 5 minutes Cook Time 9 minutes Total Time 14 minutes Servings 2 servings Author Holly Nilsson Course Dinner Cuisine American Pizza grilled cheese combines two of my all time favorite comfort foods: pizza and grilled cheese! Toasty grilled bread and an ooey gooey cheesy middle. Print Pin

Ingredients
4 slices Sara Lee Artisano Bread
3 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
2 tablespoons pizza sauce plus extra for dipping
2 tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon thinly sliced basil
pinch of dried oregano
24 thin slices pepperoni about 1″ diameter
butter
PEPPERONI CRUST (OPTIONAL)
6 slices thin pepperoni
2 tablespoons mozzarella cheese

Instructions

Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat.

Butter bread and place 2 slices butter side down in the pan. Top each slice with 1 tablespoon pizza sauce.

Layer cheese, 6 slices pepperoni, parmesan cheese, basil, oregano, remaining pepperoni and mozzarella. Top with remaining bread, butter side out.

Cook 3-4 minutes per side or just until golden.

Remove sandwich from the non-stick pan. Place 3 slices of pepperoni directly in the pan, top with 1 tablespoon mozzarella cheese. Place sandwich on top of cheese and let cook until crisp and crusted (about 1 minute). Serve.
