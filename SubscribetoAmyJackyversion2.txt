3 Pressure Cook Country Style Ribs

Close lid, then turn Venting Knob to Sealing position.

Pressure Cooking Method: Pressure Cook at High Pressure for 15 minutes + 10 minutes Natural Release

After natural releasing for 10 minutes, carefully turn the Venting Knob to Venting Position to release the remaining pressure.

Once the Floating Valve drops, open the lid carefully.

4 Apply Sauce & Broil in Oven/Airfryer

First, carefully set the country style ribs on an oven tray, then generously apply ½ cup – 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce all over to the ribs on all sides.

*Note: You can use an oven, toaster oven, Instant Pot Duo Crisp‘s “Air Fry” function or Instant Pot Air Fryer Lid for this step.

If you have a large oven, you can finish the country style ribs in one batch. If you are using the Duo Crisp lid, it will take 2 batches to brown the country style ribs.

Option 1 – Oven/Toaster Oven

Place the Country Style Ribs on an oven tray under the “Broil” function (or the highest temperature setting) for a few minutes until the BBQ sauce is caramelized.

Option 2 – Air Fryer Lid

First, remove the apple vinegar liquid from the inner pot, and add 1 cup of cold water (for easy cleanup) in the inner pot.

Then, place the airfryer basket with the tray on the middle rack in the Instant Pot (as shown in photo below).

Now, layer the cut or uncut country ribs on the airfryer tray in Instant Pot.

Then, place the air fryer lid on the Instant Pot Duo Crisp.

Air Frying Method: Press “Air Fry” button, set the temperature to 400°F and cooking time to 12 minutes.
