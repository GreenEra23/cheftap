Belgian Waffles


Minutes to Prepare: 5

Minutes to Prepare: 5

Minutes to Cook: 5

Minutes to Cook: 5

Number of Servings: 12

Number of Servings: 12

Ingredients
2 1/2 cups flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
2 beaten eggs
2 1/4 cup milk
3/4 cup vegetable oil
Or
1 1/2 cups flour
2 teaspooms baking powder
1/3 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/3 cup vegetable oil
Yield 4 waffles

Directions

In a large bowl, add the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Stir to combine.

In another bowl, mix the eggs, milk and vegetable oil well.

Add the liquid mixture to the flour mixture and stir to combine. Cook according to the waffle iron's instructions.
