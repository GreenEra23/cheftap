Surf N Turf Alfredo Sliders

- 1Lb Large Shrimp (21/25 CT- Peeled, Deveined, Cleaned, Patted Dry)
- 3/4 lb Steak (great quality/tender)

SEASONING MIX FOR THE STEAK:
- 1/2 Tsp Cracker Boy seasoning
- 1/2 Tsp Burger Seasoning
- 1/2 Tsp Seasoned Salt
- 1/4 Tsp Ground Ginger Seasoning
- 1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
- 1/2 Tsp Onion Powder
- 1/2 Tsp Salt/Pepper Blend seasoning

SEASONING MIX FOR THE SHRIMP:
- 1 Tsp Olive Oil
- 1 Tsp Old Bay Seasoning/Cajun Seasoning
- 1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
- 1/2 Tsp Onion Powder
- 1/2 Tsp Garlic Powder
- 1/2 Tsp Paprika
- Dash of Cayenne Pepper (optional)

ALFREDO SAUCE:
- 1 Tbsp Butter
- 1 Diced Medium/Large Onion
- 1/2 to 3/4 Diced Green Bell Pepper
- 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
- 1/4 Cup White Wine or Cooking Wine (Optional)
- 2 Cups Heavy Cream
- 1 Cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese (or more if you prefer)
- 1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
- 1/2 Tsp Salt
- 1/2 Tsp Garlic Powder
- 1/2 Tsp Paprika
- Parsley for Garnish

First, add 1 tsp (2.8g) garlic powder, 1 tsp (2.4g) onion powder, and 2 tbsp (30ml) regular soy sauce in Instant Pot Pressure Cooker.

Pour ½ cup (125ml) apple cider vinegar and ¾ cup (188ml) cold water in Instant Pot, then mix thoroughly.

2 Marinate Country Style Ribs

First, place 3.5 – 4 lb country style ribs into the apple cider vinegar marinade in Instant Pot.

Then, add ¼ tsp (2g) coarse kosher salt, ground black pepper, and 2 tbsp (25g) brown sugar or honey on top of the country style ribs.

Now, marinate the country style ribs for 30 minutes – 1 hour in the fridge.
