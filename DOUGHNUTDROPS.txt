DOUGHNUT DROPS


INGREDIENTS:
vegetable oil
1 1/3 cup pancake mix
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
2 tablespoon sugar
powdered sugar
cinnamon

DIRECTIONS:

DIRECTIONS:

Combine pancake mix, sugar, milk and egg.

Stir until pancake mix is moistened.

In a saucepan, heat approximately 1 in of vege oil until boiling.

Drop approx. 1 tablespoon of batter into the boiling oil.

Brown both sides, then drain on paper towels.

Sprinkle with powdered sugar and cinnamon.

Repeat.
