Baked Potato Casserole

Shredded Cheese
Bacon Bits
Chives
Salt & Pepper

Cut the potatoes into cubes and boil in water until tender.

Butter the bottom of the baking dish.

Add potatoes to the dish and pour a cup of melted butter over the potatoes, season with salt and pepper.

Top with shredded cheese, bacon bits and chives

Bake in the oven at 375 degrees for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
